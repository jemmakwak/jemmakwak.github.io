Design Goals
The mission of CS KickStart is largely echoed in nationwide programs such as Girls Who Code and other computer science education programs aimed toward womxn-identifying individuals.

I looked to Girls Who Code as a prime reference point of how a website could remain inviting and informative.

  • Produce visual assets to improve the visual experience.
