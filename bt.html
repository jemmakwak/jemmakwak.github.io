Introduction
BerkeleyTime is Berkeley's online course catalog. Under the ASUC Office of the Chief Technology Officer, our web application serves to provide easy-access to course information, real-time enrollment data, and grade distribution visualizations.
Although the designs have not been officially pushed, I am happy to talk about my design process. Feel free to reach out!
