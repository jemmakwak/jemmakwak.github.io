About BerkeleyTime
BerkeleyTime is Berkeley's online course catalog. It is utilized by every single student I've met at Cal. The website not only includes general course information like lecture and section times, but grade distributions and real-time enrollment visualizations.

The BT team has been hard at work with the UI overhaul, but the designs have not yet been pushed. If you'd like to learn more, I would be happy to talk about my process over some boba or vegan pizza! Feel free to reach out!
