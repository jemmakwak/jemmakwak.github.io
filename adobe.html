Adobe Lightroom CC
A new look at Adobe Lightroom CC.
Summer 2018 - Product Design Internship
The Challenge
Lightroom CC is the go-to photo editing application for pros in the photography industry. However, with the democratization of photo editing, new users have a difficult time beginnng to use the application, especially beginners. How might we remove the initial barriers and hesitation users have in the first minutes, hours, days of using the application? How might we use machine learning to help this overarching goal?
